🏆 Weekly Tournament Guide

What is the Tournament?

A competitive weekly season where all your matches contribute to the tournament leaderboard. Climb the ranks to earn rewards!

How Rankings Work

Tournament uses the same point system as the main leaderboard:

Win+100 pts base
Draw+40 pts base
Loss−10 pts base

Plus streak bonuses and win-ratio multipliers!

Tournament Matches

All online matches contribute to your tournament score:

  • Matchmaking battles
  • Friend matches
  • All competitive games

Weekly Reset

Tournament standings reset every Monday. Your lifetime stats remain unchanged.
